Collection Overview

Photographs depicting the Aycock High School building front exterior, teachers, and students of Aycock High School, probably class of 1940-1941, Cedar Grove, NC. Photos were originally attached to two album pages, and most of the photos have been cropped, many into abstract shapes. Thirteen (13) black and white photographic prints, various sizes ranging from approximately 5.5" x 3.5" to less than 1"x2". Photos were originally attached to two album pages, and most of the photos have been cropped, many into abstract shapes.

Biographical/Historical

Aycock High School was in Cedar Grove (Orange County), NC, and was built in 1924. Students began attending the school in January of 1925, and the last class graduated in 1963.
